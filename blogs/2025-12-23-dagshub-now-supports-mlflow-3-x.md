We’ve shipped support for MLflow 3.x on DagsHub, so you can upgrade your MLflow client and keep using the same hosted tracking server that lives inside every DagsHub repo. No self-hosting, no extra infra, no “where did my artifacts go?” moments. TL;DR: Upgrade to MLflow 3.x, point your code at your repo’s .mlflow endpoint, and keep tracking runs, artifacts, and models in one place.
